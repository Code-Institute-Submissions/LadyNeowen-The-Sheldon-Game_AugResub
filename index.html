Greetings adventurer!

You are about to embark on a fantastic and mindbuzzling journey,
to the exceedingly fun game of Stone, Paper, Scissors, Lizard, Spock!

This is not a game for the weak minded.. simpletons you have been warned!
So with no futher ado, here are the words of the renowned Sheldon Cooper
and the parameters that which the game entails and how it is meant to be played.


"Scissors cuts paper, paper covers rock, rock crushes lizard, lizard poisons Spock,
Spock smashes scissors, scissors decapitates lizard,
lizard eats paper, paper disproves Spock, Spock vaporizes rock,
and as it always has, rock crushes scissors."

Live long and prosper.
